Recycling as a Business

At BD Waste, we are passionate about reducing plastic pollution and creating a sustainable environment. We have a proven track record of shipping 40-60 tons of PET plastic bales and flakes per month to the European market, and we are committed to maintaining the highest quality standards in all our operations. Our products meet the strictest quality requirements and are ideal for the manufacturing of new plastic products.

We take pride in our flexible pricing range, which we have designed to accommodate the unique needs of our customers. Additionally, we conduct our business with the utmost transparency and ethical standards, which have helped us build a reputation as a reliable partner in the plastic waste management industry.
